Genetic Drift Simulation

A visual simulation of genetric drift and its effect on elephants' tusks

Go to github

Where are the tusks?

I did this project back in my senior year, when I was teaching myself C++ and SFML.
The simulation aims to observe the evolution of a randomly generated population of elephants, some with tusks, some without. When male and female meet, they may reproduce ; depending on your simulation, you may end up with a population of elephants with higher amount of tusked individuals or the other way around.
